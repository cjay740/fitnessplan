SUPPLEMENTS
Optional but helpful. Diet alone can achieve results.
🥛
Whey Protein
25–30g protein per scoop. Take post-workout within 30 min. Best way to hit protein targets without overeating.
✅ Top Priority ₹2000–3000/mo 1–2 scoops/day
⚡
Creatine Monohydrate
Increases strength 10–15%, fastest muscle builder. Most researched supplement — completely safe.
✅ Highly Recommended ₹500–800/mo 5g/day anytime
🌟
Multivitamin
Fills nutritional gaps. Especially Vit D, B12, Zinc — most Indians are deficient in all three.
✅ Recommended ₹300–500/mo 1 with breakfast
🐟
Fish Oil (Omega-3)
Reduces inflammation, speeds recovery, joint health. Essential for anyone lifting weights regularly.
✅ Recommended ₹400–700/mo 2 caps with meals
☀️
Vitamin D3
80% of Indians are deficient. Critical for testosterone, muscle function, and immunity.
✅ Recommended ₹200–400/mo 2000 IU daily
☕
Pre-Workout / Caffeine
Black coffee works perfectly and it's free! If using pre-workout, cycle 4 weeks on, 1 week off.
⚠️ Optional Coffee = free 30 min pre-workout
